Chicken Noode Soup

Class, comforting, and a cure-all for whatever ails you. Super delicous soup that only takes 30 MINUTES!

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup carrots, peeled and sliced thin (baby carrots preferred)
  • 1 cup celery, sliced thin
  • 1 cup sweet Vidalia or yellow onion, peeled and diced (about 1 medium onion)
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 64 ounces (8 cups) chicken broth, plus more if desired
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp fresh thyme (or 1/2 tsp dried)
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 12 ounces egg noodles
  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken (use rotisserie chicken to save time)
  • 2 Tbsp parsley
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste

Directions

  1. Add oil to a large dutch oven or stockpot. Heat over medium-high heat to warm.
  2. Add the carrots, celery, onion, and saute for about 7 minutes, or until vegetables begin to soft. Stir intermittently. Add the garlic and saute for another 1 or 2 minutes.
  3. Add the chicken broth, bay leaves, thyme, oregano, pepper, and bring to a boil. Allow mixture to boil gently for about 5 minutes or until vegetables are fork-tender.
  4. Add the egg noodles and boil mixture for about 10 minutes, or until noodles are soft and cooked through.
  5. Add the chicken, parsely, optional lemon juice, and boil 1 or 2 minutes, or until chicken is warmed through. Remove bay leaves and serve immediately
