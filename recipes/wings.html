Smoked Chicken Wings

Ingredients

  • 6 pounds chicken wings
  • Your favorite bbq rub
  • Olive Oil

Directions

  1. Separate wings into drumettes and wings, if necessary.
  2. Pat wings dry
  3. Place wings in a container, add olive and rub over chicken.
  4. Let wings rest for at least an hour.
  5. Heat smoker to a temperature between 225-250 F.
  6. Place wings over indirect heat.
  7. Add wood for the smoke
  8. Smoker for 2 - 2 1/2 hours, maintaining a constant smoker for at least 1 1/2 hours, the chicken should have an internal temperature of 160.
  9. Place directly on grill to crisp, about 5 minutes per side.
  10. Let rest for 10 minutes, sauce and serve.
